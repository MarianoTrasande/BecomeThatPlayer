About Me

I'm Mariano Trasande, a professional football scout specializing in the technical, tactical, physical, and mental analysis of players.

I completed a series of professional scouting certifications through the International Professional Scouting Organisation (IPSO), all endorsed by YMCA Awards, including:

    I also hold the Safeguarding in Sports certification from High Speed Training (UK), which focuses on athlete welfare and professional standards when working with players of all ages.

    Additionally, I completed the Performance Analysis programme at ATFA (Asociación de Técnicos del Fútbol Argentino), strengthening my understanding of game models, tactical structures, and performance evaluation.

    I gained applied professional experience collaborating with Southport FC and Coventry City, working within their scouting departments and contributing to internal player evaluations.

    Today, I bring together more than eight years of experience in professional scouting and performance analysis, working at Dodici Sports Management, one of the largest football agencies in South America.

    Do you guarantee trials, contracts, or club placements?

    No. I provide professional scouting evaluations, guidance, and development support. I am not a placement agency and I cannot guarantee that any player will receive trials, contracts, scholarships, or club interest as a result of the evaluation.

    Can this evaluation help me get noticed?

    Yes — but indirectly. The goal is to help you understand how a scout interprets your game, what traits stand out, what gaps limit your projection, and how to present yourself better.

    What do you need from me?

    A Wyscout profile or a video link (YouTube / Google Drive / Veo / match footage). The review is built from your actual match contexts, not highlight compilations.

    How long does it take?

    48–72 hours after receiving your video or Wyscout link.

    What if I’m not sure which service to choose?

    You can send your video first or contact me through email/LinkedIn — I’ll tell you honestly what I think is useful for your situation before you book anything.
